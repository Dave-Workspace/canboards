Understanding Language Tests

01

IELTS (International English Language Testing System)

IELTS is one of the most popular English language proficiency tests for international students and immigrants. It assesses your abilities in Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking.

  • Widely accepted by universities globally.
  • Two modules: Academic (for study) and General Training (for immigration/work).
  • Available in paper-based and computer-delivered formats.
02

CELPIP (Canadian English Language Proficiency Index Program)

CELPIP is a Canadian English language proficiency test designated by IRCC for permanent residence and citizenship applications, and professional designation.

  • Fully computer-delivered, Canadian-focused content.
  • Two versions: General (for immigration) and General LS (for citizenship).
  • Integrated speaking and writing components.
