Our Story

Since our inception, Canboards has been on a mission to make immigration simpler, smarter, and more accessible. Born from firsthand experience navigating complex processes, our founders saw an opportunity to remove barriers and create a clear, supportive path to Canada.

Today, Canboards combines licensed RCIC expertise with cutting-edge technology, earning recognition for its innovation, transparency, and results. Our growth reflects a simple truth: when you put people first and processes second, success follows.

Join The Canboards Team

Our Mission

To democratize access to Canadian immigration by merging expert legal guidance with digital tools making the process faster, and more accessible for everyone.

Our Vision

To become the most reliable, tech-enabled immigration platform globally redefining how people move, connect, and grow in Canada.

From the Founder's Desk

Canboards began with a single belief that immigration should feel empowering, not overwhelming. As an immigrant and entrepreneur myself, I witnessed firsthand the hurdles people face while pursuing opportunities in Canada. That's why I built Canboards: to offer a smarter, simpler, and more secure path forward. We don't just file applications; we build futures. And behind every dashboard login or eligibility check, there's a human story we care deeply about. At Canboards, we're not just your consultants we're your co-pilots

-Hardik Patel,
Founder of Canboards
