Understanding the Canadian Student Visa

What is a Study Permit?

To apply for a Canadian student visa, you'll need to meet specific criteria and provide a range of documents.

  • Letter of Acceptance (LOA) from a Designated Learning Institution (DLI).
  • Proof of sufficient funds to cover tuition and living expenses.
  • Proof of identity (passport, photos).
  • Statement of Purpose (SOP) explaining your study intentions.
  • Clean criminal record and good health (medical exam may be required).

Key Application Requirements

You must apply for an extension before your current study permit expires. Key documents include proof of enrollment and financial capability.

  • Proof of enrollment from your Designated Learning Institution (DLI).
  • Proof of sufficient funds for your extended stay.
  • Valid passport and current study permit.
  • Explanation for the need for extension (e.g., program delay).
  • Compliance with previous study permit conditions.
